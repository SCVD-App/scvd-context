## Audio Architecture

### Dual Analyser
- **VOX** — narrowband bandpass: HP 200Hz → LP 3000Hz → analyser
- **ROOM** — unfiltered raw signal for ambient metering
- **MIN_VOX_RMS** = 0.06 — rejects backing track bleed, requires close vocal
- **silencer** — source → gain(0) → destination (prevents Android speaker routing)
- **sampleRate** = 48000 (Bluetooth A2DP native, reduces profile switching)
- **latencyHint** = 'playback'
- **getUserMedia constraints** — echoCancellation: false, noiseSuppression: false, autoGainControl: false, channelCount: 1

### Known Bluetooth Issue
Android switches from A2DP to HSP/HFP profile when mic is armed — OS-level behaviour, cannot be fully prevented. Manifests as slight compression and volume boost on Bluetooth speakers. Wired headphones or phone speaker recommended for best results. Document in onboarding tip next session.
