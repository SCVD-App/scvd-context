## 🎯 Architecture Direction — Reference-Track Subtraction (Audio Engine v3)

**The core idea:** Once AudD identifies a song, we know its tempo, key, and acoustic fingerprint. Use that knowledge to actively filter out signal matching the known track, isolating the voice rather than relying purely on amplitude gating.

**Why this matters:**
- Solves Lou's ambient-music-scoring problem (subtract the known track regardless of volume)
- Solves Marie's distance problem (same mechanism)
- **Opens up headphone support** — even a faint bleed-through is enough for AudD identification; reference subtraction then does the heavy lifting on scoring, not raw mic amplitude
- This is a genuine differentiator, not just a bug fix — frame as a headline feature for v3, not a patch

**Honest scope:** This is a multi-session build, not a quick fix. Needs its own dedicated project day.
